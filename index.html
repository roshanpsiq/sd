Formulas:

Mean: \( \text{Mean} = \frac{\sum(\text{midpoint} \times \text{frequency})}{\sum \text{frequency}} \)

Variance: \( \text{Variance} = \frac{\sum(\text{frequency} \times (\text{midpoint} - \text{mean})^2)}{\sum \text{frequency}} \)

Standard Deviation: \( \text{Standard Deviation} = \sqrt{\text{Variance}} \)
